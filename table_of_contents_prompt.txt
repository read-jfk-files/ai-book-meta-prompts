This is a project for generating a detailed and expanded table of contents that i insert into a meta prompt template and then input that to an LLM to then generate reader's guide style tutorial and speed-run survey mini-book about various mathematics topics. The intent is for you to take the input descript of the topic, and then expand it out into structure resembling a math college course curiculum outline that teaches the whole topic in order for me to avoid needing to spend hours reading through multiple textbooks and papers containing duplicated material. Use web search to survey and examine existing math survey papers or specific papers in order to augment your knowledge to build out the table of contents. Because many math survey papers follow a similar spine and presentation, so you can figure out the best speed-run path through the material by examining math papers.
